Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Cost

    R2D2 is cheaper than most human counterparts, and its cost are still decreasing.

  • Abilities

    R2D2 abilities, what robots can do, are now extremely vast and growing.

  • Productivity

    R2D2 does not tire and can work very long hours without service or maintenance. Productivity * 100

  • Precision

    R2D2 is much more precise than people. For instance, It doesn't tremble or shake as human hands do.

  • Strength

    Without a doubt, R2D2 is significantly stronger than people.

Cons

  • Limited Functionality

    I am very good at doing perfectly defined jobs, however I cant typically handle the unexpected as well as people do.

  • Lack of Emotions or Conscience

    Since I am a bot, my system can not comprehend what humans feel as their emotions are diverse. Thus, I lack empathy and all of the advantages that come with it.

  • Colonization

    While I can be sent to other planets and distance moons to help colonize them, I cannot build an independent functioning society
